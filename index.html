Complete Workflow ⚙️

Follow these steps to save your parts list and upload it for processing.

Critical Instructions: This tool ONLY works for McMaster-Carr and Metal Supermarkets. You MUST save the HTML from the SHOPPING CART page. It will not work on a product page.

  1. One-Time Setup: Go to the "Bookmarklet Setup" tab.
    Click and drag the green "Save Clean HTML" button to your browser's bookmarks bar.
  2. Add parts to your cart on the vendor's website.
    Go to either the McMaster-Carr or Metal Supermarkets website and add all desired items to your shopping cart.
  3. Go to your shopping cart and click the bookmarklet.
    Once on the cart page, click the "Save Clean HTML" button in your bookmarks bar. A cleaned-up HTML file of your cart will save to your computer's Downloads folder.
  4. Return here and go to the "Uploader" tab.
    Use the navigation bar at the top to switch to the uploader form.
  5. Upload the cart file.
    Choose the correct vendor, select the HTML file you just downloaded from your cart, and click "Submit Request".
