Complete Workflow ⚙️

Follow these steps to save a product page and upload it for processing.

  1. Go to the "Bookmarklet Setup" tab.
    Click and drag the green "Save Clean HTML" button to your browser's bookmarks bar. You only need to do this once.
  2. Navigate to a vendor product page.
    Go to a product page on a site like McMaster-Carr or Metal Supermarkets.
  3. Click the bookmarklet.
    Click the "Save Clean HTML" button you just added to your bookmarks. A cleaned-up version of the page will be saved to your computer's Downloads folder.
  4. Return here and go to the "Uploader" tab.
    Use the navigation bar at the top to switch to the uploader form.
  5. Upload the file.
    Choose the correct vendor, select the HTML file you just downloaded, and click "Submit Request".
